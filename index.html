Youngjung Kim

Currently I am a Senior Researcher at Agency for Defense Development (ADD), South Korea. Our team works on developing automatic algorithms for scene understanding, remote sensing, and multi-agent control.

I got my Ph.D. degree in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University in 2018 (supervisor: Prof. Kwanghoon Sohn). Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree from the Yonsei University in 2013.
